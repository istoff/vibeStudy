Exam Study Game

Game Rules:

  1. Select an exam topic and target score
  2. Roll the dice to move around the board
  3. Landing on a '?' space triggers a question
  4. Correct answers earn 5 points, wrong answers lose 2
  5. Special spaces: '+' gives bonus points, '-' deducts points
  6. First to reach the target score wins!
Player: You
